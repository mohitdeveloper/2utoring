How we keep you safe

Safeguarding is at the heart of our platforms' design, incorporating a number of mechanisms to keep you safe and provide total peace of mind.

Digital classroom sessions on 2utoring with under 18s are multi-factor recorded, including each individual participant's screen, any active webcams, and audio channels. These recordings are encrypted and safely stored in the cloud for total protection and compliance. All tutors that have a green 'Enhanced DBS' status badge on their profile have been checked and validated by the 2utoring safeguarding team. This is unrivalled safety and security, on a truly revolutionary platform.
